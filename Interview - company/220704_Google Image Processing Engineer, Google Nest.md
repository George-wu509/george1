Minimum qualifications:  
 

- Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Imaging Science, Color Science, or equivalent practical experience.
- Experience with image processing in a scripting language (e.g., Matlab, Python, etc.).
- Experience in academic digital image processing or equivalent experience.  
      
     

Preferred qualifications:  
 

- Doctorate degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Imaging Science, Color Science, or relevant fields.
- Experience with C++ and C.
- Experience with Typescript.
- Knowledge of one or more of the following: CMOS image sensors, Auto White Balance algorithm, Auto Exposure, or color science.
- Knowledge of ISP algorithms.
- Programming skills in one of the following: MATLAB, Python.  
      
     

About The Job  
  
As an Image Processing Engineer at Google Nest, you will be able to deliver Image Quality (IQ) on Google Nest camera products. We engage in product development cycle from the early stage, to ISP performance optimization, and then to the final shipment of the cameras. We work closely with many cross-functional teams such as hardware, product management, platform, etc.  
  
The Google Nest team focuses on hardware, software, and services for the home, ranging from Nest thermostats to Nest smart displays. The Google Nest team develops, designs, and develops new technologies and hardware to make users’ homes more helpful. Our mission is the helpful home: to create a home that cares for the people inside it and the world around it.  
  
Responsibilities  
  
 

- Evaluate ISP Image Quality performance.
- Produce product IQ tuning.
- Develop tools for IQ tuning and evaluation.
- Develop productivity tools for test automation.
- Develop ISP/3A algorithms.
